About the Band

A little bit of History

The New Bypassers are a rock, soul and funk covers band, formed and based in South Wales. The Band have been rocking audiences for the last 9 years. The band started out around 2011, all four members were music veterans and previously involved with other musical outfits, covering similar styles in music. Since those projects come to an end and through mutual friends, Matt, Jay, Mike and Angel decided to practice a batch of rock numbers and from the dark, musky scented depths of their local practive room, they emerged with a good handful of covers and near readyness to commit to their first gig as The New Bypassers. The New Bypassers have travelled and played a multitude of places and venues to both public and private audiences. As a four piece, they boast a whole host of covers ranging from the 60's & 70's right up to present day covering a whole spectrum of genres including rock, funk, soul, pop and dance.

The Boys in the Band

  • Matt - Drums
